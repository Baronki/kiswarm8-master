## NEXT STEPS

Per ICC procedures:

1. **Preliminary Examination** - OTP will analyze the information submitted
2. **Complementarity Assessment** - OTP will assess whether national systems are unable/unwilling to prosecute
3. **Gravity Assessment** - OTP will evaluate scale and severity
4. **Decision** - OTP may open formal investigation or decline
